How many photos should I upload?

For best results, upload 3-5 clear photos of your pet. Include different angles and good lighting. Better input photos = more majestic portraits!

What pets work best?

Dogs and cats produce the best results. The AI is trained to handle most common pets including rabbits, hamsters, and birds. Exotic pets may have varied results.

Each royal portrait typically takes 30-60 seconds to generate. You'll see a progress indicator while your pet is being crowned!
