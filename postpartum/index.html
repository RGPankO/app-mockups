2. The Problem

  • Baby apps track feeding and sleep, but ignore the mom's physical recovery.
  • No consistent support for mental health check-ins or PPD indicators.
  • Identity rebuilding and confidence milestones are missing from current tools.

3. Core Features

  • Mom's daily check-in for mood, energy, and pain.
  • Physical recovery tracking for bleeding and pelvic floor health.
  • Mental health screening focused on PPD indicators.
  • Self-care reminders with tiny, doable steps.
  • Mom-only milestones to celebrate recovery wins.
