Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any equipment?

No! FloorStrong is designed to work with no hardware, sensors, or gadgets. Just you and your body. Find a quiet place and follow the guided exercises.

Which program should I start with?

If you're new to pelvic floor exercises, start with the Beginner program (4 weeks). If you're postpartum, the Postpartum Recovery program is designed specifically for you.

How often should I exercise?

The app recommends sessions based on your program. Most programs suggest 3-5 sessions per week, with rest days in between for recovery.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your exercise data never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access if you reinstall the app or get a new device.

Should I consult a doctor first?

Yes, especially if you're postpartum, have pelvic pain, or any health conditions. FloorStrong provides general exercise guidance, not medical advice.
