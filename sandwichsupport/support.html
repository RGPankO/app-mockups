Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "sandwich generation" caregiver?

The sandwich generation refers to adults (typically 40s-60s) who are "sandwiched" between caring for aging parents AND raising their own children. It's an incredibly demanding role, and SandwichSupport is built specifically for you.

What does the daily check-in do?

The daily wellness check-in helps you track how you're really doing. It monitors stress levels, sleep, and self-care over time so you can spot patterns and prevent burnout before it happens.

What is the AI Coach?

The AI Coach is like having a supportive friend who understands caregiving. It offers practical advice, validation, and coping strategies based on caregiver wellbeing research. It's not therapy, but it helps in the daily moments.

What are Boundaries?

The Boundaries feature helps you set and maintain healthy limits. Caregivers often overcommit. This feature gives you scripts and strategies for saying no, delegating, and protecting your time.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your wellbeing data and care notes never leave your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access if you reinstall the app or get a new device.
