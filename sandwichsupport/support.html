Frequently Asked Questions

What is the "sandwich generation"?

People caring for both aging parents AND children at the same time. It's exhausting, often invisible, and SandwichSupport is built specifically for you.

What's the Wellness Check-in?

A quick daily prompt to assess your energy, stress, and emotional state. Over time, patterns emerge that help you recognize when you need to prioritize self-care.

How do Boundaries work?

Set healthy limits with pre-written scripts and reminders. The app helps you establish and maintain boundaries with family members ‚Äî a critical skill for preventing burnout.

What's the AI Coach for?

A judgment-free space to vent, process emotions, or get advice. The coach understands caregiver challenges and offers evidence-based strategies for managing stress.

Does the app work offline?

Most features work offline! All check-ins, boundaries, and care notes are stored locally. The AI Coach needs internet for some responses.

Is my data private?

Completely. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers and no way to see your caregiving journey. See our Privacy Policy for details.
