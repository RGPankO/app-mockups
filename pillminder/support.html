Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my medication data stored?

All your medication schedules and history are stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot access your sensitive health data.

Why aren't my reminders working?

Make sure notifications are enabled for PillMinder in your device Settings. On iOS, also check that "Time Sensitive Notifications" are allowed so reminders break through Do Not Disturb.

How do I add a new medication?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen. Enter the medication name, dosage, and set your reminder times. You can also add optional notes like "take with food."

Can I track multiple medications?

Yes! Add as many medications as you need, each with their own schedule and reminder times.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data to save your medication list. Import it on your new device using Settings ‚Üí Import Data.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Restore Purchase". This will restore your premium access using your Apple ID or Google account.

Tips for Better Adherence

  • Consistent times: Take medications at the same time each day
  • Use the snooze: Snooze a reminder if you need a few minutes
  • Mark as taken: Log doses immediately to track accurately
  • Check history: Review your adherence in the calendar view
