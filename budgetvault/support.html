Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes! BudgetVault works 100% offline. Your budget is always available, no internet required.

Why no bank sync?

Privacy. Bank connections require sharing credentials and transaction data with third parties. BudgetVault keeps your financial data on YOUR device only.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Unlike apps that collect and sell spending data, we never see your transactions. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I back up my data?
