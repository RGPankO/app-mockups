Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your flashcards and study progress are stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot access your data.

Can I sync my flashcards to a new phone?

Since data is stored locally only, it doesn't automatically sync. However, you can export your decks from Settings and import them on your new device.

How does spaced repetition work?

FlashForge uses the SM-2 algorithm to schedule reviews. Cards you find easy appear less often, while difficult cards appear more frequently. This optimizes your study time for maximum retention.

How do I create a new deck?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen to create a new deck. Give it a name and start adding cards with front (question) and back (answer) content.

Can I add images to my flashcards?

Yes! When creating or editing a card, tap the image icon to add photos from your camera roll.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → "Restore Purchase". This will restore your premium access using your Apple ID or Google account.

Study Tips

  • Study daily: Short, consistent sessions beat long cramming
  • Be honest: Rate cards accurately for better scheduling
  • Keep cards simple: One concept per card works best
  • Use images: Visual cues improve memory
  • Review before bed: Sleep helps consolidate memories
