Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes! WildernessRx is designed for use in remote areas with no cell service. All content is downloaded and stored locally on your device.

Where is my data stored?

All data (bookmarks, notes, emergency contacts) is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot access your information.

How do I find emergency protocols quickly?

Use the search function or browse by category (Wounds, Fractures, Environmental, Bites & Stings, etc.). You can also bookmark frequently needed guides for quick access.

Can I add my own emergency contacts?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Emergency Contacts to add local emergency numbers, ranger stations, or personal emergency contacts for your trip.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Restore Purchase". This will restore your premium access using your Apple ID or Google account.

Before Your Trip

  • Download everything: Make sure all content is cached before going offline
  • Add emergency contacts: Include local ranger stations and emergency services
  • Bookmark key guides: Quick access to the protocols you might need
  • Take a course: This app supplements but doesn't replace wilderness first aid training
