Executive Summary

MindLoad is a calm AI companion for working moms. For $9/month, it converts brain dumps into shared plans, removing the mental burden of constant remembering and re-planning. The product blends AI with real family context to make daily life feel lighter.

  • Invisible labor: Planning, scheduling, and remembering are unpaid and unseen.
  • Household management burden: The cognitive load sits with one person, often the mom.

  • Smart daily prioritization: Balanced plans that protect energy.
  • AI meal planning: Dinners + instant shopping lists.
