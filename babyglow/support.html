Frequently Asked Questions

How many photos should I upload?

For best results, upload 5-10 clear photos. Include different angles and good lighting. Better input photos = better generated images!

How long does generation take?

Each image typically takes 30-60 seconds to generate. You'll see a progress indicator while it's processing.

Where are my generated images saved?

Images are saved directly to your device's photo gallery. You can also access them in the app's Gallery tab.

What happens to my photos?

Your photos are temporarily processed for generation, then deleted from our servers. We don't keep copies or use them for AI training. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I get a refund if I don't like the results?

Refunds are handled through the App Store. If you're unsatisfied, contact Apple Support through your purchase history.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your credits if you reinstall the app or get a new device.
