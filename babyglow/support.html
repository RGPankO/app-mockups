Frequently Asked Questions

How many photos should I upload?

5-10 clear photos of each parent work best. Include different angles and good lighting. Avoid sunglasses, hats, or heavily filtered images.

How long does generation take?

Usually 2-5 minutes depending on server load. You'll get a notification when your images are ready!

Are the images accurate predictions?

BabyGlow uses AI to create artistic interpretations based on parent features. They're fun keepsakes, not scientific predictions. Actual genetics is far more complex!

What happens to my photos?

Your photos are used only to generate images, then deleted within 24 hours. We never use them for AI training or share them with anyone. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I get a refund?

If you're unhappy with your images, contact us within 7 days. We'll either regenerate your pack or process a refund through Apple/Google.
