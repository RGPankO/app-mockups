Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I record?

Make sure you've granted microphone permission. Go to Settings ‚Üí WhisperMemo ‚Üí Microphone ‚Üí Enable.

Where are my recordings stored?

All recordings are stored locally on your device only. They never leave your phone.

Can I export my recordings?

Yes! Tap any recording and use the share button to export to other apps or save to Files.

Will I lose my recordings if I delete the app?

Yes. All recordings are stored locally. If you delete the app, your recordings are permanently removed. Export important memos before uninstalling.

Does WhisperMemo work offline?

Yes! WhisperMemo works 100% offline. No internet connection is ever required.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access on any device signed into the same Apple ID/Google account.

Can I sync between devices?

Not currently. WhisperMemo is designed for maximum privacy, which means no cloud sync. Export recordings manually if needed.
