About Voice Recordings

Your voice is deeply personal. WhisperMemo was built for people who want to capture thoughts without worrying about who might be listening.

100% Local Storage: All voice memos, transcriptions, and notes are stored exclusively on your device. We have no servers that receive or store your recordings.
