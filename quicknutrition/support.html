Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for a food?

Just start typing in the search bar! QuickNutrition searches through 10,000+ foods from the USDA database. Results appear instantly as you type.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! QuickNutrition works 100% offline. The entire food database is stored on your device. No internet connection needed ‚Äî perfect for camping, traveling, or shopping.

How do I save favorite foods?

Tap the heart icon on any food to save it to your favorites. Access your favorites from the Favorites tab for quick lookup.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your data never leaves your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access if you reinstall the app or get a new device (using the same Apple ID).

Can I request a food to be added?

QuickNutrition uses the USDA FoodData Central database. If a food is missing, email us and we'll try to include it in a future update.
