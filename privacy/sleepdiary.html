Privacy Policy

SleepDiary

Effective Date: February 2026

Our Promise: SleepDiary stores ALL your sleep data locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to our servers — because we don't have any.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✅ 100% Local Storage — All sleep data stays on your device
  • ✅ No Accounts — No sign-up, no passwords
  • ✅ No Cloud Sync — Your sleep patterns never leave your phone
  • ✅ No Analytics — We don't track your behavior
  • ✅ No Ads — No advertising networks, no tracking

Information We Collect

From your device: Nothing. We have no servers to receive data.

From app stores: Apple/Google may provide anonymized statistics (download counts, crash reports) that contain no personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your sleep logs, notes, and patterns are stored using:

  • AsyncStorage — Encrypted at rest by iOS/Android
  • All data remains on-device only

Information Sharing

We do not share your information because we don't have it. Your sleep data never leaves your device.

Your Rights

  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: Export your sleep history for personal use
  • Control: You have complete ownership of your data

Third-Party Services

SleepDiary does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

We do not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.
