Privacy Policy

WhisperMemo

Effective Date: February 2026

Our Promise: WhisperMemo processes ALL your voice recordings locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to our servers — because we don't have any.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✅ 100% On-Device Processing — Voice transcription happens locally
  • ✅ No Cloud Upload — Your voice never leaves your phone
  • ✅ No Accounts — No sign-up, no passwords
  • ✅ No Analytics — We don't track your behavior
  • ✅ No Ads — No advertising networks, no tracking

Information We Collect

From your device: Nothing. We have no servers to receive data.

From app stores: Apple/Google may provide anonymized statistics (download counts, crash reports) that contain no personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your voice memos and transcriptions are stored using:

  • Local File Storage — Audio files on your device only
  • AsyncStorage — Encrypted at rest by iOS/Android
  • On-device speech recognition (no internet required)

Information Sharing

We do not share your information because we don't have it. Your voice recordings never leave your device.

Your Rights

  • Delete: Delete individual memos or uninstall the app
  • Export: Share or copy your transcriptions
  • Control: You have complete ownership of your recordings

Third-Party Services

WhisperMemo uses on-device speech recognition provided by iOS/Android. No audio is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

We do not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.
