Privacy Policy

BudgetVault

Effective Date: February 2026

Our Promise: Your financial data is YOUR business. BudgetVault stores everything locally on your device. We have no servers, no cloud, and no way to access your data.

Why Privacy Matters for Finance Apps

Your spending habits reveal a lot about you. Unlike other budget apps, BudgetVault was built from the ground up to never know your financial data exists.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✅ 100% Local Storage — All expenses stay on your device
  • ✅ No Accounts — No sign-up required
  • ✅ No Cloud Sync — Nothing leaves your phone
  • ✅ No Bank Connections — We never ask for bank access
  • ✅ No Analytics — We don't track behavior
  • ✅ No Ads — No advertising networks

Information We Collect

From your device: Nothing. We have no servers.

From app stores: Apple/Google may share anonymized statistics that contain no personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your expenses, budgets, and categories are stored using:

  • SQLite — Local database, encrypted by iOS/Android
  • AsyncStorage — Preferences and settings

Data Export

You can export your data to CSV or PDF at any time. This export stays on your device unless you choose to share it.

Information Sharing

We don't share your data because we don't have it. Your financial information never leaves your device.

Your Rights

  • Export: Download your data as CSV/PDF anytime
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Control: Full ownership of your financial data
