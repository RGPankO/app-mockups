Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between standard and scientific mode?

Rotate your device to landscape for scientific mode, or tap the "SCI" button to toggle.

How do I access the unit converter?

Tap the converter icon in the toolbar or swipe left on the calculator screen.

Where is my calculation history?

Swipe down on the display or tap the history icon to see previous calculations. Tap any result to reuse it.

How do I switch between degrees and radians?

Tap the "DEG" or "RAD" indicator at the top of the scientific mode screen to toggle.

What are the memory functions?

M+ adds to memory, M- subtracts, MR recalls, MC clears. The "M" indicator shows when memory has a value.
