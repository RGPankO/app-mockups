Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work offline?

Yes! WildernessRx works 100% offline. All first aid guides are stored locally — perfect for remote areas.

How do I find specific emergencies?

Use the search bar or browse by category (Wounds, Burns, Bites, etc.).

Can I bookmark important guides?

Yes! Tap the bookmark icon on any guide for quick access later.

Is this a substitute for medical training?

No. This app provides reference information only. We recommend taking a wilderness first aid course.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access if you've purchased previously.

How do I delete my data?

Bookmarks and preferences are stored locally. Uninstalling the app removes all data.
