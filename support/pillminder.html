Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a medication?

Tap "Add Medication" and enter the name, dosage, and schedule. Set up reminders to never miss a dose.

Does this work offline?

Yes! PillMinder works 100% offline. Reminders work locally without internet.

Will I get reminders?

Yes! Set reminder times for each medication. Make sure notifications are enabled in your phone settings.

Can I track my medication history?

Yes! The History tab shows when you took each medication and tracks adherence.
