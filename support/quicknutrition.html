Frequently Asked Questions

How do I look up a food?

Type any food name in the search bar. Results appear instantly from the built-in database of 60+ common foods.

Does this work offline?

Yes! QuickNutrition works 100% offline. All food data is stored locally on your device. No internet required.

How do I save favorites?

Tap the heart icon on any food to add it to your favorites. Access favorites from the Favorites tab.

Can I request new foods?

Email us with food suggestions and we'll consider adding them in future updates.
