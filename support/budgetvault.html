Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an expense?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Enter the amount, select a category, add an optional note, and save.

Can I create custom categories?

Yes! Go to Settings → Categories to add, edit, or delete your expense categories.

Does this sync between devices?

No — BudgetVault stores all data locally on your device for privacy. There is no cloud sync. Use the export feature to backup your data.

How do I export my data?

Go to the Export tab and choose CSV or PDF. The file will be saved to your device or you can share it directly.

How do I set a budget limit?

Go to Settings → Budget Limits. Set monthly limits for each category and you'll see progress bars as you spend.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchase to restore premium access on this device.

I lost my data. Can you recover it?

Unfortunately, no. Your data is stored only on your device and we have no access to it. We recommend regular exports for backup.
