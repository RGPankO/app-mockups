Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create flashcards?

Tap "New Deck" to create a deck, then add cards with front (question) and back (answer).

Does this work offline?

Yes! FlashForge works 100% offline. Study anywhere without internet.

How does spaced repetition work?

Cards you get wrong appear more often. Cards you know well appear less frequently. This optimizes your learning.

Can I import/export decks?

Yes! Export your decks as backup files and import them on another device.
