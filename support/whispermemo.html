Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record a voice memo?

Tap the record button to start. Tap again to stop. Your memo is automatically transcribed on-device.

Does transcription work offline?

Yes! WhisperMemo uses on-device speech recognition. No internet required.

How accurate is the transcription?

Accuracy depends on audio quality and background noise. Speak clearly for best results.

Can I edit transcriptions?

Yes! Tap any memo to edit the text. Your changes are saved automatically.
