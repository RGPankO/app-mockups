Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a vehicle?

Tap "Add Vehicle" and enter make, model, year, and current mileage. You can track multiple vehicles.

Does this work offline?

Yes! AutoLog works 100% offline. Track maintenance anywhere without internet.

How do I log maintenance?

Select a vehicle and tap "Log Maintenance". Record oil changes, tire rotations, repairs, and more.

Will I get maintenance reminders?

Yes! Set mileage or time-based reminders for regular maintenance items.
