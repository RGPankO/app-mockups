Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of therapy programs are included?

IntimateHeal includes evidence-based programs for common concerns: pain during intimacy, low desire, postpartum recovery, and reconnecting with your body. Each program has guided exercises and education.

Is my progress private?

Completely. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to see your therapy journey. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How does the AI Coach work?

The AI Coach provides judgment-free support and guidance. You can ask questions, discuss concerns, or get encouragement. It's like having a supportive friend who understands intimate health.

Does this replace seeing a therapist?

IntimateHeal is a supplement, not a replacement. For serious concerns, we always recommend working with a licensed sex therapist or pelvic floor specialist. The app can support your journey between sessions.

Does the app work offline?

Most features work offline! All programs and education are available without internet. The AI Coach requires connection for some responses.
