Frequently Asked Questions

Why track ADHD symptoms with cycle phases?

Research shows hormone fluctuations significantly affect ADHD symptoms in women. By tracking both together, you can identify patterns ‚Äî like symptoms worsening before your period ‚Äî and plan accordingly.

What is the Pomodoro timer?

The Pomodoro technique uses focused work intervals (typically 25 minutes) followed by short breaks. It's proven effective for ADHD brains. FocusHer's timer is designed specifically for ADHD-friendly focus sessions.

How do I find patterns in my data?

After tracking for at least one cycle, go to the Insights tab. FocusHer analyzes your symptom and focus data against your cycle phases to show when you're most/least focused.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your ADHD symptoms and cycle data never leave your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can this diagnose ADHD?

No. FocusHer is a self-tracking tool, not a diagnostic tool. If you suspect you have ADHD, please consult a qualified healthcare provider for proper assessment.
