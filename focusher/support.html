Frequently Asked Questions

Why is FocusHer designed for women?

ADHD presents differently in women and can be affected by hormonal cycles. FocusHer helps you track both ADHD symptoms and your cycle to spot patterns traditional apps miss.

How do I use the Pomodoro timer?

The Focus Timer uses the Pomodoro technique: 25-minute work sessions with short breaks. Tap Start to begin a session. The app will notify you when it's time for a break.

What's the Pattern Insights feature?

After logging symptoms for a few weeks, FocusHer shows you correlations between your cycle phase and ADHD symptoms. Many women find their focus and energy vary predictably throughout their cycle.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! FocusHer works 100% offline. All your data stays on your device.

Is this a diagnosis tool?

No. FocusHer is for personal tracking only. If you suspect you have ADHD, please see a healthcare provider for proper evaluation.
