Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my row counter?

Tap the counter display to reset to zero, or long-press to set a specific number.

Can I track multiple projects?

Yes! Create multiple counters and switch between them from the main screen.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your counts and projects are permanently removed.

Does CraftCount work offline?

Yes! CraftCount works 100% offline. No internet connection is ever required.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access on any device signed into the same Apple ID/Google account.

Can I sync between devices?

Not currently. CraftCount is designed for maximum privacy, which means no cloud sync. Your data stays on your device only.
