Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your sleep data is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot access your data. This means your sleep patterns stay completely private.

Can I sync my data to a new phone?

Since data is stored locally only, it doesn't automatically sync to new devices. However, you can export your data from Settings and import it on your new device.

How do I start a sleep entry?

Tap the "Start Sleep" button when you go to bed. In the morning, tap "Wake Up" to complete your entry. You can also manually add entries if you forget.

What does the sleep quality rating mean?

The 1-5 star rating is your subjective assessment of how well you slept. Over time, this helps identify patterns in your sleep quality.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Restore Purchase". This will restore your premium access using your Apple ID or Google account.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Clear All Data" to erase all sleep entries. Alternatively, deleting the app removes all data permanently.
