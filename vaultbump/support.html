Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my data 100% local?

VaultBump was designed for maximum privacy. In a world where pregnancy data can be subpoenaed or leaked, we chose to never store your data on any server. Your pregnancy journey stays on your phone only.

What does the Panic Button do?

The Panic Button instantly and permanently deletes ALL data from the app. This cannot be undone. Use it if you need to quickly erase your pregnancy tracking data.

What if I get a new phone?

Since we don't store data in the cloud, your data cannot be transferred between devices. This is by design ‚Äî maximum privacy means no sync capability.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! VaultBump works 100% offline. It never needs an internet connection because your data never leaves your device.

Is this medical advice?

No. VaultBump is for personal tracking only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions during pregnancy.
