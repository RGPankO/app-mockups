Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't VaultBump have cloud sync?

By design. Cloud sync requires servers, and servers can be hacked, subpoenaed, or compromised. VaultBump keeps your pregnancy data 100% on your device for maximum privacy.

What does the Panic Button do?

The panic button immediately and permanently deletes ALL data from the app. This cannot be undone. Use it if you need to quickly remove all pregnancy tracking data.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! VaultBump works 100% offline because it has no servers. Your data never requires an internet connection.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes. Data is stored using iOS/Android secure storage mechanisms. Additionally, you can set an app-specific passcode for extra protection.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Restore Purchase. This will restore your premium access if you reinstall the app or get a new device (using the same Apple ID).

Can I export my data?

No ‚Äî this is intentional. Data export could be used to compromise your privacy. VaultBump is designed so your data never leaves your device.
