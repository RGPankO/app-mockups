Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your vehicle data is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot access your information.

How do I add a fuel fill-up?

Tap the "+" button, select "Fuel," enter the odometer reading, gallons/liters, and price. AutoLog calculates your MPG/L per 100km automatically.

Can I track multiple vehicles?

Yes! Add multiple vehicles from the home screen. Each vehicle has its own fuel history, maintenance log, and statistics.

How do I set maintenance reminders?

Go to a vehicle ‚Üí Maintenance ‚Üí Add Reminder. Set reminders based on mileage (e.g., oil change every 5,000 miles) or date (e.g., registration renewal yearly).

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data to download your logs as CSV files for spreadsheets or tax purposes.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí "Restore Purchase". This will restore your premium access using your Apple ID or Google account.

Tips for Accurate Tracking

  • Fill completely: Log fill-ups when you fill the tank completely for accurate MPG
  • Log immediately: Enter data at the pump while it's fresh
  • Track all expenses: Include maintenance, repairs, insurance for true cost of ownership
  • Review monthly: Check your statistics to spot trends
