Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your hydration data is stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot access your information.

How much water should I drink?

The app calculates a personalized goal based on your weight. A common guideline is about 8 glasses (64 oz / 2L) per day, but this varies by activity level, climate, and individual needs.

How do I log water?

Tap the "+" button or use the quick-add buttons for common amounts (glass, bottle, etc.). You can also customize your quick-add amounts in Settings.

Can I set reminders?

Hydration Tips

  • Start your day: Drink a glass of water right when you wake up
  • Carry a bottle: Keep water visible as a reminder
  • Drink before meals: A glass before eating aids digestion
  • Watch for signs: Thirst, dark urine, and fatigue signal dehydration
  • Adjust for activity: Drink more during exercise and hot weather
